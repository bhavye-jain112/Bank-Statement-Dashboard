# Bank-Statement-Dashboard
Power BI dashboard for analyzing a society's income, expenses, and financial transactions. It provides insights into spending patterns, income sources, fiscal-year performance, and budget variance, helping administrators monitor finances, improve transparency, and support informed financial decision-making.

**Business Insights:**
+ Total income (₹26.90 Cr) slightly exceeded expenses (₹26.82 Cr), resulting in a positive surplus of ₹7.48 Lakh.
+ Financial activity was highly concentrated in a few major income and expense categories, which together accounted for the majority of transactions.
+ FY 2023–24 recorded the highest income and expenditure levels, indicating peak financial activity during the period.
+ Income and expenses generally moved together across years, suggesting effective budget utilization and financial planning.
+ Annual financial activity peaked in 2023, with both income and expenses crossing ₹100M.
+ The society maintained a positive balance in most fiscal years, reflecting overall financial stability.
+ Transaction volumes were significantly higher on the credit side (17,782 transactions) compared to the debit side (1,167 transactions).
+ Recent years show a decline in overall financial activity compared to the 2023 peak, indicating reduced income and expenditure levels.
+ The relatively small surplus compared to total turnover suggests that most available funds were utilized for society operations and activities.
+ Year-wise analysis highlights consistent financial management with no major deficits during the selected period.
